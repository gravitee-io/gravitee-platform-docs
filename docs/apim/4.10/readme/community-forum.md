### This is a community forum

The majority of people answering questions, providing feedback, and supporting this forum are **volunteers**. Bear this in mind when posting questions and interacting with fellow community members.

If someone answers your question, thank them and mark the answer as the solution. If you see something useful or helpful, give it a like. Share the love—we all appreciate recognition.

We're grateful to our community members for helping each other. We all want to see everyone succeed. Be kind, be thoughtful, and follow the [Code of Conduct](http://community.gravitee.io/t/code-of-conduct/) guidelines.

### Have we met before?

Welcome to the club! We'd love to know more about you. Stop by the [Hello category](http://community.gravitee.io/c/hello-salut-hola-n-h-o-namaste-privet-ciao/) and tell us a bit about yourself.

### I need some help!

Do your own research first. Have you checked the documentation? Have you checked out the [FAQs](http://community.gravitee.io/c/frequently-asked-questions)? Did you search the forum? Your question may have already been answered.

Post in the correct category. This speeds up getting an answer and makes it easier for others to find similar questions in the future.

Be detailed in your question. What version are you using? What environment is involved? How did you get to your current challenge? What have you tried already? What were you expecting to happen? This level of detail helps the community help you.

Posting a config file or log snippet? **Remove or blank out** any sensitive information, such as **usernames**, **passwords**, and **IP addresses**.

Got that answer? Great! Thank those who helped you and mark the answer as the solution.
